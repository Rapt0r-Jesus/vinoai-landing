Features

Everything you need to know about your wine

From a simple photo to a complete wine profile in seconds — no wine expertise required.

📷 AI Label Scanner

Take a photo of any wine bottle label. Claude Vision AI reads and interprets the label instantly — name, vintage, producer, and region extracted in seconds.

🍇 Complete Wine Profile

Get the full picture — grape varieties, appellation, tasting notes, and food pairing suggestions. Data sourced from GrapeMinds API and enriched by Claude AI.

🗄️ Personal Wine Cellar

Save your scanned wines to your personal cellar. Add your own rating (1–5 stars) and tasting notes. Your collection is stored locally — no account needed.

About

The story behind VinoAI

I've always been a wine enthusiast, but every time I ordered a bottle at a restaurant, I found myself completely unable to remember which wine I'd loved once I left. The name, the château, the vintage — all gone.

"What if there was a Shazam for wine?"

That simple question led to VinoAI. An app that lets you point your phone at any bottle, scan the label, and instantly get everything you need to know — and remember it forever in your personal cellar.

VinoAI was built as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School by Dorian Oufer, First Year Student, 2025–2026.
